class="fade-in"Impacts on India

class="fade-in" India is highly vulnerable to climate change. Heatwaves are becoming more frequent and intense, affecting millions of people.

class="fade-in" Monsoon patterns are becoming irregular, leading to both droughts and floods in different parts of the country.

class="fade-in" Rising temperatures and water shortages are serious concerns for agriculture and drinking water supply.
