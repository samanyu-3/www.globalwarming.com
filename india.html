Impacts on India

India is highly vulnerable to climate change. Heatwaves are becoming more frequent and intense, affecting millions of people.

Monsoon patterns are becoming irregular, leading to both droughts and floods in different parts of the country.

Rising temperatures and water shortages are serious concerns for agriculture and drinking water supply.
